The Real TCO of Big Data

  • By Vihar Chokshi
  • In financial
  • Posted Dec 3, 2016
Summary: The Total Cost of Ownership of a 1PB Big Data solution over 5 years can exceed $37M. Infrastructure requirements, licensing costs, Operations & Maintenance (O&M) costs, and growth are often timed underestimated. The biggest risk to budgetary adherence is the scarcity and wide range of salary requirements for qualified data scientists.
